Overview

I just started my PhD in mathematics under the supervision of Alice Pellet-Mary and Benjamin Wesolowski, focusing on lattices and their applications to cryptography. I completed my undergraduate studies at Université Paris Cité (formerly Paris Diderot), where I earned a double BSc in Mathematics and Computer Science. I obtained my master's degree in pure mathematics from Sorbonne Université. My research interests include Number Theory, Algebraic Geometry, and Algebraic Cryptography.
