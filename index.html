About Me

I am a PhD student in mathematics at the Institut Mathématiques de Bordeaux (IMB), under the supervision of Alice Pellet-Mary and Benjamin Wesolowski. My research focuses on lattices and their applications to cryptography.

I hold a double BSc in Mathematics and Computer Science from Université Paris Cité and a master's degree in pure mathematics from Sorbonne Université. My research interests also include Number Theory, Algebraic Geometry, and Algebraic Cryptography.
